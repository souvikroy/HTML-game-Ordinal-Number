How to Play

Welcome to Ordinal Odyssey! This game will help you learn ordinal numbers (1st through 10th) through fun racing challenges.

What You'll Learn:

  • How to recognize and use ordinal numbers
  • Understanding positions in a sequence
  • Solving problems using ordinal numbers

Controls:

  • Click or tap on objects to select them
  • Drag and drop items to place them
  • Follow GridBot's instructions to complete each level
